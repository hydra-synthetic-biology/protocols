Hydra Medium (100L)
===

Inputs
---

0. Nanopure H2O
0. CaCl2 - 14.7g
0. MgCl2 - 2.03g
0. NaHCO3 - .303g
0. KNO3 - 4.2g
0. MGSO4 - -.96g

Outputs
---

0. Hydra Medium

Equipment
---

0. Scale
0. Stir Paddle
0. Nalgene Tank

Procedure
---
0. Weigh out each salt
0. Place weighed salt into Nalgene tank.
0. Add 100L Nanopure H20
0. Stir with Stir paddle until all salts are dissolved.
